the first Epigram.


If Boreas once expand his windy womb,
That the Infant in the world alive may come,
His prowess will the Heroes all outdo
In art, in hand, in mind, and body too.
Think him no Caeso, nor abortive brood,
Nor yet Agrippa, for his star was good.

the first Epigramme Epigram.


If Boreas once expand his windy wombe womb,
That th' the Infant in the world alive may come,
His prowesse prowess will the Heroes all outdoe outdo
In art, in hand, in mind, and body too.
Thinke Think him noe no Cæso, nor abortive brood,
Nor yet Agrippa, for his starre star was good.
